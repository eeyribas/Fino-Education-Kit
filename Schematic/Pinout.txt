Connecting Modules with Arduino;

8x8 Dot Matrix Arduino Shield - Arduino
- Din = 12
- Clk = 11
- Cs = 10

Arduino XY Joystick Module - Arduino
- Vrx = A0
- Vry = A1

Buttons (Touch Sensor) - Arduino
- (Button Up) SIG = 6
- (Button Left) SIG = 5
- (Button Right) SIG = 4
- (Button Down) SIG = 3

LEDs - Arduino
- Yellow Led = 8
- Red Led = 9

Accelerometer Sensor (3-Axis Accelerometer ADXL345) - Arduino
- SCL = A5
- SDA = A4

Buzzer - Arduino
- Pin = 7
